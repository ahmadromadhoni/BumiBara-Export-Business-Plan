# Export Business Plan: BumiBara (Premium Coconut Shell Charcoal Briquettes)

An end-to-end strategic export business plan developed for the **BumiBara** brand, specializing in the international trade of Indonesian premium coconut shell charcoal briquettes (**HS Code 4402.20**). This project integrates data-driven market research, cross-border digital operations, maritime logistics for dangerous goods, and continuous quality improvement frameworks.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
* **Brand Name:** BumiBara (Eco-Friendly Briquettes)
* **Commodity:** Premium Coconut Shell Charcoal Briquettes
* **HS Code:** 4402.20
* **Production Base:** Jombang, East Java, Indonesia
* **Academic Baseline:** 28 peer-reviewed journals, trade databases, and institutional references.

---

## 🛠️ Integrated Operational Modules & Documentation
This business plan strictly follows the **7n1 Arrows of Export Management** framework, comprehensively documented across multiple operational components:

### 1. Market Research & Trade Data Analysis
* **Global Demand Mapping:** Statistical evaluation using Trade Map (2022–2025) identifying main international buyers (Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Lebanon, Türkiye, Russian Federation).
* **Quality Standardization:** Laboratory parameter mapping aligning product specs (Moisture: 3.12%–3.71%, Ash Content: Max 2.5%, Fixed Carbon: 93.14%) with SNI 01-6235-2000 standards.

### 2. Branding & Digital B2B Operations
* **Brand Identity & Rebranding:** Full packaging layout development, official logos, and professional corporate stationary optimized via Canva.
* **B2B Infrastructure:** Deployment of an official WordPress Web Interface and active merchant sourcing utilizing international B2B platform tools (Alibaba RFQ Simulation).

### 3. Trade Financing & Compliance Documentation
* **Financial Risk Mitigation:** Comparative risk matrix for global payment methods, specializing in secure Letter of Credit (L/C) banking workflows.
* **Export Cargo Documents:** Completed legal templates including Packing List, Commercial Invoice, Shipping Instruction, and Certificate of Origin (COO/e-Form D).

### 4. Supply Chain Logistics & Dangerous Goods Management
* **Maritime Freight Planning:** Multi-modal logistics cost structuring (Warehousing, Inland Trucking, Forwarding Fees, and Ocean Freight to Umm Qasr & Jeddah) via PT Global Sarana Transport.
* **Dangerous Goods Safety (Class 4.2):** Standard compliance integration using Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS), Self-Heating Test (SHT) certification from independent labs, and Dangerous Goods Declaration (DGD) under the IMDG Code protocol.
* **Customs Clearance:** Step-by-step electronic processing for Export Declaration (PEB) managed directly through the Indonesia National Single Window (INSW) portal.

### 5. Quality Assurance & Risk Mitigation
* **ISO 31000 Alignment Risk Matrix:** Structured mitigation strategies for raw material supply fluctuations, operational machine down-times, administrative penalties, and customs red-channel (Jalur Merah) contingencies.
* **Deming Cycle (PDCA Framework):** Continuous improvement workflows applied across Customs Administration and Jombang Supply Chain/Warehouse Operations to assure zero data anomalies and optimum moisture control.
